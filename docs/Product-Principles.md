# Product Principles

The core beliefs that shape every DueWatch decision.

## Every screen answers three questions
1. **What needs my attention?**
2. **What should I do next?**
3. **Can DueWatch handle this for me?**

If a screen doesn't answer these, it doesn't ship.

## Sell outcomes, not features
Founders don't want an "invoice table" — they want to get paid on time without chasing. Copy, layout, and priorities lead with the outcome, not the mechanism.

## AI is the employee, not the feature
DueWatch's AI is framed as a capable teammate that handles the follow-up work, not a bolt-on "AI feature." The goal is delegation, not another dashboard to operate.

## Calm confidence, not alarming dashboards
The product should feel reassuring and in-control — warm tones, quiet surfaces, clear next steps — never a wall of red alerts. Overdue money is stressful; the interface should reduce that stress, not amplify it.
