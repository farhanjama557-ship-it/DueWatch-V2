# Roadmap

Current priorities, in order.

1. **Wire Morning Brief "Draft Reminder" buttons** — clicking a Draft Reminder on an overdue invoice opens its detail panel, ready to send. *(Done in this session.)*
2. **AI-generated reminder drafts via Claude API** — replace the static draft template with a Claude-generated message tuned to the client's history and tone tier.
3. **Real email sending** — actually deliver reminders (transactional email provider), not just log them.
4. **Autopilot V1 (rules-based)** — automatic reminder escalation on a schedule (first → firm → final notice) without manual clicks.
5. **Landing page deployment** — public marketing page.
6. **Founder testing** — onboard real founders and gather usage data (see the `events` table).
